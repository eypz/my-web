Welcome to Izumi-v3

Izumi-v3 is a powerful and versatile multi-device WhatsApp bot designed to enhance your WhatsApp experience. Easily deployable on Heroku, Railway, Render, Replit, and Codespaces.

Izumi Support

Features

Izumi-v3 offers a range of features to make your WhatsApp interactions more efficient and enjoyable:

  • Multi-device support
  • Automated messaging
  • Customizable commands
  • 24/7 uptime
  • User-friendly interface
How to Deploy

Deployment

Deploy Izumi-v3 effortlessly on your preferred platform:

  • Heroku: Simple and quick deployment.
  • Railway: Reliable and scalable solution.
  • Render: Fast and easy setup.
  • Replit: Ideal for development and testing.
  • Codespaces: Perfect for GitHub integration.
